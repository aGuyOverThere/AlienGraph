# USS Omaha Sphere UAP

## Overview

The USS Omaha Sphere UAP footage, released by [[ğŸ‘¤ Jeremy Corbell]] in 2021, shows a spherical object captured by multiple sensor systems aboard the USS Omaha off the coast of San Diego in July 2019. The incident is particularly significant as it documents a UAP demonstrating trans-medium travel capabilities, ending with the object appearing to enter the water.

## Technical Details

### Recording Information
- **Date**: July 15, 2019
- **Location**: Off the coast of San Diego
- **Platform**: USS Omaha (LCS-12)
- **Equipment**: 
  - FLIR Systems
  - Radar
  - Visual observation
- **Duration**: Multiple recordings
- **Released**: May 2021

### Object Characteristics
- Perfect spherical shape
- Estimated 6 feet in diameter
- Solid appearance
- No visible propulsion
- Trans-medium capability
- High-speed travel
- No thermal signature
